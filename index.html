About Us









Shankhnaad is anannual inter-collegiate techno-cultural and literary festival organized by the students of "Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Handicapped, Kanpur". Being one of the major events it is being organized with the aim to develop interest, conviction to participate and encourage the hidden talents and innovation among the students. Along with having the largest number of blood donors from a college within kanpur we are also deliberately working on Unnat Bharat Abhiyan under which we have adopted five villages. We have also been actively involved in the SWACHH BHARAT ABHIYAN and strive top romote many more multiple social causes through Shankhnaad.
