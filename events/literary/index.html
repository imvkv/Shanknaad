Quiz : The Prashnottari









This will be a team based event. We will give entries to the participants in a team of 3 members.
Each team will be given a buzzer and the team which presses the buzzer first after the question is asked will get the chance to answer first. If their answer is incorrect then the question will be passed to the next team.
The team will be awarded 5 marks for each correct answer and no marks will be deducted for a wrong answer.
And the end of the quiz, the maximum scoring team will be declared as the winner.

An Eristic (Debate competition)









This will be a debate competition having three (3) phases. In each phase a new topic will be given by the coordinator. These topics will be pre-decided and provided to the participants one (1) week before the commencement of event. The participants have to prepare for both in favor as well as against the motion. The coordinator will randomly decide whether a participant will be in the affirmative team or in the opposing team. The language for the debate will be English. The participants will be divided into two teams, affirmative and opposing.
Debate opens with the affirmative team presenting their arguments followed by a member of opposing team. This pattern will be repeated until the last member of each team has presented his views.
Finally, each team gets an opportunity to counter the arguments of their opponent.
Each phase will be of twenty (20) minutes duration. After the end of each phase 1-2 participants will be eliminated from both the teams on the basis of number of valid arguments made by them. There will be a judging panel of three (3) judges. The judges will be taking notes of the arguments made by each team and after the completion of phase 3, the best team wins the debate.


    Advisory:

  1. Both the teams should not deviate from the topic of debate.
  2. Any kind of short-tempered behaviour or use of abusive words will result in immediate disqualification of that particular participant.
  3. Only logical points with proper evidence to support will be considered valid.
  4. The arguments made should be short but descriptive.
  5. Argument made once should not be repeated otherwise it will result in deduction of points from the total score of that team.
  6. One participant cannot speak continuously for more than 45 seconds.


Note: Eliminated participants will not be considered as winners if their team wins the debate.









Bibliophile / The Sameekshak









The participants can choose any novel of their choice and prepare a review of that. They have to present their review in front of the audience as well as the judging panel. The review should contain


  • An introduction to the author(s), including the author's title and place of work, and some indication of who the author is.
  • A brief summary of the novel along with an introduction to the main theme of the novel and description of its content.
  • An evaluation of the novel's merits, usefulness, and special contributions, along with shortcomings you think are necessary to point out.
  • The review should be short but descriptive. Each participant will be given 10 minutes for his review.
  • The review should introduce the audience to the book's content and focus on the subject of the book being reviewed.

This event will be conducted in three (3) phases.
The first round will be a general knowledge quiz (Aptitude Test).
The second round will be a picture perception & group discussion test.


  1. Some blurred images will be shown to the participants and they will have to build a story based on that image.
  2. Those images will be shown for a limited period of time i.e. 30 seconds and then 5 minutes will be given to them to write down their story on a provided sheet.
  3. After that the participants will be clubbed in groups of 10 members. And they will be asked to discuss their story and come to a conclusion.


The final phase of the competition will be a mock interview round.
There will be an interview committee of 3 judges. First the candidates have to submit their resume a day before the commencement of the event.
These resumes will also be evaluated by the judges. After the submission of resumes the candidates will be notified about the timing of interview. The interview committee will judge the candidates on the basis of their overall personality, linguistic skills, speaking ability and subjective knowledge.
Out of all candidates only three (3) will be awarded as 1st, 2nd and 3d.









Tech Shayar









The participants will have to write as well as recite a poem based on engineering lifestyle. They are also allowed to share their personal views, opinions and experience related to engineering.


  • Each poem must be an original piece performed by the participant/group.
  • Performances will be timed and should not exceed three (3) minutes of time duration. There will be a random draw for performance order.
  • There will be no restriction for language. Participants can use whatever language they are comfortable with but it should be understandable by the audience.
  • Heckling or disrupting of another performer's performance results in disqualification.
  • Use of abusive words during performance will result in immediate disqualification.









Supermemer









This event will be conducted in three stages.
The very first round will be a quiz which will be based on memes.
The second round will be a meme making competition. The participants will be shown an image or they will be given some kind of clue and we will ask them to make a meme related to that in a limited duration of 5 minutes.
The final round will be conducted on stage. Only 5 participants will be selected from round 2. These participants will be called upon the stage one by one they will play the main round which will be a game similar to 'KBC'.
After the end of last round, the participants will be ranked on the basis of number of correct answers given by them and the winner will be awarded.









Sell It Out









In this event the participants will be given some random products and they will be asked to convince the audience to purchase it by telling them about the qualities and benefits of the product.
The participants will be given a time period of 5 minutes for advertising.
At the end of the event the participant with maximum number of buyers will be declared as the winner.









Spill the words









This event will be conducted in two rounds.
The first one will be a team based round. We will give entries to the participants in a team of 3 members.
Each team will be given a buzzer and the team which presses the buzzer first after the question is asked will get the chance to answer first. If their answer is incorrect then the question will be passed to the next team.
